Latest Update · July 2026

A New Season.

For the past two years, I’ve had the privilege of serving with Driven to Encourage Ministries alongside Eric Helms and Dylan Russ — working within the public school ministry system and starting discipleship groups along the way. That season shaped me more than I could have orchestrated myself.

I’m beyond excited to share that I’ve taken the next step of faith: stepping fully into ministry with Fellowship of Christian Athletes, working directly with coaches and athletes. Getting to walk out my calling through sports has God’s plan written all over it, and I’m all in!

Stepping into full-time ministry means raising significantly more support than I’ve ever needed before — and I’m asking for your partnership to help make it possible.

The Ministry

About FCA

Fellowship of Christian Athletes is a non-denominational Christian sports ministry founded in 1954. FCA exists to lead every coach and athlete into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ and His church — working through campus ministry, camps, and communities across the country.

Learn More at FCA.org

The Missionary

About Jeremy

JW

Jeremy Womack spent two years serving with Driven to Encourage Ministries, preaching the gospel in public schools and leading discipleship groups. In 2026, he stepped into full-time ministry with Fellowship of Christian Athletes, serving coaches and athletes across Cherokee County. He’s passionate about walking alongside the next generation as they grow in their faith and their game.
